Milena Korol Audio Production Portfolio

About the module:

The goals of this module were to teach us about:

  • Sound Theory: Characteristics of sound, Harmonic series, Basic music theory, and Music appreciation
  • Voiceover Scripting
  • Digital Audio: Intro to analogue and digital recording, DAWS, Audio editing, Multi-tracking, Loop based composition, Synchronising with visuals, DSP effects, Time stretch and pitch shifting, Beat synching, Beat grids, Warping, and Audio file types and compression
  • Publishing Audio
  • Copyright
  • Sample Projects: Commercial advertisements, and Music mashup

Assignment 1: Ad for SETU Arena

In this assignment we had to Write a script, Record, and Edit a 30 second ad promoting SETU Arena

Assignment 2: Ad for SETU Peer to peer mentoring

In this assignment we had to Write a script, Record, Produce a backing track, and Edit a 30 second ad promoting the SETU Peer to peer mentoring program

Assignment 3: Ad for Smarter travel

In this assignment we had to Write a script, Record, Produce a backing track, and Edit a 45 second ad promoting TFI smarter travel

Assignment 4: Music mashup and Portfolio

In this assignment we had to produce a 60 second mashup using sampling in Ableton Live
